# Problem Statement: SignSpeak Smart Glove

## The Challenge

Over **72 million people worldwide** rely on sign language as their primary mode of communication. However, the vast majority of the general population cannot understand sign language, creating a significant communication barrier in everyday interactions.

### Current Limitations

1. **Camera-Based Solutions**
   - Require specific lighting conditions
   - Need direct line of sight
   - Pose privacy concerns in private spaces
   - Limited by camera angle and distance
   - Cannot work in low-light environments

2. **Accessibility Issues**
   - Most assistive devices are expensive
   - Require constant internet connection
   - Lack real-time processing capabilities
   - Not portable or wearable

3. **Communication Gap**
   - Deaf/Hard-of-Hearing individuals face daily communication challenges
   - Healthcare, education, and service workers often cannot communicate effectively
   - Emergency situations become more dangerous without proper communication

## Our Solution

**SignSpeak Smart Glove** is an affordable, standalone, privacy-first wearable device that:

- ✅ Translates hand gestures into natural spoken sentences in **real-time**
- ✅ Works without cameras or video recording
- ✅ Operates wirelessly via Bluetooth
- ✅ Provides context-aware sentence generation using AI
- ✅ Offers low-latency performance (<500ms end-to-end)
- ✅ Runs on battery power for portability

## Target Impact

- **Primary Users**: Individuals who use sign language as their primary communication method
- **Secondary Users**: Healthcare professionals, educators, service workers who interact with the Deaf community
- **Long-term Goal**: Bridge the communication gap and promote inclusivity

## Success Metrics

- **Accuracy**: >85% gesture classification accuracy
- **Latency**: <500ms from gesture to speech
- **Usability**: <1 minute setup time
- **Battery Life**: 6-8 hours continuous operation
- **Range**: Up to 10 meters Bluetooth connectivity
